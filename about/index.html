About Us

Technology drives exploration to the Moon, Mars and beyond. MYC (MyCompany) Space Technology Mission Directorate develops transformative space technologies to enable future missions. As MYC embarks on its next era of exploration.

During the MYC New Moon program of the 2006 and ‘2020s, MYC sent nine missions to the Moon. Six of them landed astronauts safely on the surface, the only times humans have visited another world.

MYC is led by Administrator Wooden Joe, MYC’s 12th administrator. Before joining MYC, Wooden served in the Open collaboration space for 3 years.
